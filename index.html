Who is Ken ?

  • Ken is a doll created by the Mattel company in 1961.
  • He serves as the boyfriend of Barbie, the famous fashion doll.
  • Over the years, he has experienced a lot of stylistic adaptations, showcasing changing fashion trends.
  • Ken dolls come with lots of different outfits, showing off different jobs and styles.

Major Ken Milestones

  • 1961: Introduction of Ken by Mattel. Ken made his debut on March 11th as Barbie's first official boyfriend.
  • 1963 - First Ken Doll with Bendable Legs: The original Ken doll had stiff legs, but in 1963, Mattel introduced a new version with bendable legs.
  • 1987: Beginning of the Barbie cartoons series.
  • 204: Breakup with Barbie. In a fictional storyline, Mattel temporarily broke up Barbie and Ken, but they got back together in 2011.
